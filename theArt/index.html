WHAT
IS CALLED ART?

Art, also called (to distinguish it fro onter art forms) visual art, a visual object or experience continuously created through an expression of skill or imagination.

7 TYPES OF ART

The seven different art forms are Painting, Sculpture, Literature, Architecture, Theater, Film and Music. However, back in the day, the seven different art forms were called liberal Arts, consisting of Grammer, Logic Rhetoric, Arithetic, Geometry, Astronomy, and Music.
